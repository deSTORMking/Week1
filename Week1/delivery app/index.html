Click Labs

It is one of the prominent players in on-demand economy space, providing iOS and Android-based applications. With 1000 plus clients that handle more than 3 million transactions a month, ClickLabs is providing solutions for customized software development in more than 160 countries. Its primary goal is to empower businesses on the move. Clicklabs has further entities that scale up the tech revolution around the world.
